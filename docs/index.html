About noidea

noidea is a Git companion that makes your commits better and funnier. Born out of late-night coding sessions and the chaos of development, it aims to add a touch of personality to your Git workflow. With smart AI-powered features and a judgmental Moai watching over your commits, it transforms the serious business of version control into something more entertaining.
